Balasmic Dressing

A silky tart balsamic dressing that is well balanced and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 Cups Balsamic Vinigar
  • 1 3/4 Cups Olive Oil
  • 1 Head of Garlic
  • 2 Table Spoons of Dijon Mustard
  • 1 Table Spoon of Dried Basil
  • 1 Table Spoon of Dried Oregeno
  • 1 Tea Spoon of Salt
  • 1 Tea Spoon of Ground Black Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup of Sugar

Equipment

  • Handheld Immersion Blender
  • 2 Quart High Rimmed Container
  • 1 Quart Storage Container

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients into the high rimmed container.
  2. Use immersion blender to blend ingredients together making sure incorperate air into the ingredients.
  3. Incorperate air by moving the immersion blender close to the surface producing bubbles.
  4. Once mixture is fully blended and thickened in viscosity, you are done!
  5. Recipe should make one quart of dressing and be stored in the fridge.

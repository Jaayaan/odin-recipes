Apple Bacon Bleu Cheese Salad

A filling and funky salad that has the right balance of flavors to never get boring!

Ingredients

  • One Small Head or Half a Large Head of Romaine Lettuce
  • 1/4 of a Cucumber
  • 1/4 Cup Balsamic Dressing
  • 1/4 of a Apple
  • 1/4 Cup Bacon Bits
  • 1/4 Cup Bleu Cheese Crumble
  • 1/4 Cup Candied Pecans

Equipment

  • A knife
  • A Cutting Board

Instructions

  1. Begin by chopping the romaine, cucumber, and apple into bite size pieces.
  2. Then layer each ingredient in the the order they are listed on the ingredient list.
  3. Enjoy!
